
Enterprise architecture involves the analysis, design, planning, and implementation of enterprise analysis to effectively execute business strategies. It aids organizations in structuring IT projects and policies to achieve desired business outcomes, maintain agility and resilience amidst rapid changes, and adapt to industry trends and disruptions. This process, also known as enterprise architectural planning, utilizes architecture principles and practices.

Modern EA strategies have expanded to encompass the entire business, beyond just IT, ensuring alignment with digital transformation strategies and technological advancements. Particularly beneficial for large businesses undergoing digital transformation, EA focuses on integrating legacy processes and applications to create a cohesive environment.
